Upgrading to CommandLineUtils 3.0

    Upgrading McMaster.Extensions.Hosting.CommandLine

    In order to fix #294, McMaster.Extensions.Hosting.CommandLine 3.0's dependency on Microsoft.Extensions.Hosting was lowered to a dependency on Microsoft.Extensions.Hosting.Abstractions. In some cases, this could cause your app to fail to compile when you upgrade with errors.

    Symptom

    After upgrading to 3.0, your app fails to compile with

    error CS0246: The type or namespace name 'HostBuilder' could not be found"

    Resolution

    Add a dependency on Microsoft.Extensions.Hosting
